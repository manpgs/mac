NAME

securityuploadd — Keychain Metrics Uploader

DESCRIPTION

securityuploadd Lightweight on-demand daemon to upload keychain and keychain syncing metrics. Split out from the main keychain daemons for security reasons: we do not wish to give our daemons access to the network.

October 10, 2017 macOS 14.3
