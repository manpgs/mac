NAME

attach_automation_image — attach and initialize disk images for automation_trampoline(8)

SYNOPSIS

attach_automation_image automation_image_url

DESCRIPTION

attach_automation_image attaches and initializes the disk image at automation_image_url on behalf of automation_trampoline(8). It is not intended for general use, and its behavior is not documented. Use hdiutil(1) instead.
